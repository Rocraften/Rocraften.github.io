About Me

Hey — I’m Rocraften. I build things you can play with: Minecraft addons, texture packs, Roblox content, and small games. I like messy creativity more than perfection. If it's fun, useful, or a little weird, I want to try it.

I do 3D models, pixel art, and Python projects when the mood hits. This place is a tidy map of that chaos — feel free to poke around.

Skills

  • Minecraft — addons, behavior packs, texture packs, skins, and experiments for education.
  • Roblox — building, UI, and Lua basics for fun experiences and small games.
  • Python — games, tools, and tiny utilities (Pygame stuff included).
  • 3D Modeling — Blender & Blockbench for props, renders, and in-game assets.
  • Pixel Art — occasional pixel work when the project calls for it.
