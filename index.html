About Me

Hey — I’m Rocraften. I make stuff you can mess with: Minecraft addons, texture packs, Roblox bits, and small games. I like making things that feel fun and useful, not perfect.

I model in Blender and Blockbench, dab in pixel art sometimes, and write small Python projects when I’m bored. This site is a tidy map of that mess — look around, grab anything that looks cool.

My Skills

  • Minecraft — addons, behavior packs, texture packs, skins, and MC:EDU experiments.
  • Roblox — building, UI, basic Lua scripting, and modeling.
  • Python — small games (Pygame), tools, and utilities.
  • 3D Modeling — Blender & Blockbench for props and in-game assets.
  • Pixel Art — occasional pixel work when the project needs it.
